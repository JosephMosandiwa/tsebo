    CONNECT WITH ANY DEVICE
    JUST ADD MUSIC
    Iconic in shape, elegant in appearance and gorgeous in sound, Beoplay A9 is a one-point music system capable of handling all of your music needs. With the A9 all you need is a digital music source and the music system will take care of the rest - and make beautiful sounds flow through your home with ease.

     

    Connect using AirPlay, Chromecast built-in, DLNA or Bluetooth 4.0 and you will be playing your music in a matter of seconds. And built-in access to Spotify Connect, Deezer and TuneIn gives you access to their millions of songs, playlists and radio stations.


    Beoplay A9 features the BeoLink Multiroom technology, which unites your B&O PLAY and Bang & Olufsen products into one wireless system.
     
    You can play different music in different rooms – or let one song play on all your speakers throughout the home.
     
    Read more about Chromecast built-in.
    Read more about the BeoLink Multiroom.

    B&O PLAY X Kvadrat
    Fabulous Fabrics

    The collaboration between B&O PLAY and Kvadrat kicked off with our Beoplay A6 covers, and is continued with a series of gorgeous wool-blend covers for Beoplay A9.

    The fabric is custom designed, in collaboration with Kvadrat, who are widely recognized for their design textiles.

    We’re proud to continue the work we do with Kvadrat, as the end result brings the very best qualities from the textile industry to our audio product.

    The fabric brings you the very best of both worlds; acoustic transparency and superior aesthetics. This is done using delicate and sophisticated multi-colored threads.
